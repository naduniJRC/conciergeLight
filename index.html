Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a website to use ConciergeAI? add

No. Our Zero-Presence onboarding builds your entire digital footprint through a simple WhatsApp or phone conversation. We create your booking page, collect your services and prices, and deploy your AI agent — all in under 60 seconds.

Which languages does ConciergeAI support? add

We support 50+ languages natively — not via translation. This includes regional dialects like Yoruba, Tagalog, Tamil, and Gulf Arabic, with correct grammar and cultural tone. Your AI automatically detects the customer's language and responds in kind.

How does billing work for international businesses? add

We support local payment methods including M-Pesa, GCash, PIX, UPI, and all major cards via Stripe. Invoices are issued in your local currency where available.

Can I switch plans or cancel at any time? add

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with a single message to your AI — no forms, no phone calls. Your data is yours and can be exported at any time.
